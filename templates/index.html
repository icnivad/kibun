Welcome to MoodToolkit

This website helps you keep track of how different activities affect your mood.

How to use this site:

Step 1: go to Choose Activity
Step 2: Enter your current feeling and mood, and an activity you'd like to do.
Step 3: After completing the activity, enter your new mood.
Step 4:You can see a list of your activities at Activity History and you can see a summary of how different activities affect your mood at Activity Summary.
You'll have to enter a few different activities before you'll see any data here though. Also, please signup if you want to be able to save your data!
